To provide high-quality, compassionate healthcare services to our community while advancing medical knowledge through research and education. We are committed to treating every patient with dignity, respect, and the highest standard of medical care.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in the region, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and exceptional patient experience. We strive to create a healthier community through comprehensive medical services and preventive care.

1985

City General Hospital was founded with 100 beds and a vision to serve the community.

1995

Expanded to 200 beds and added specialized cardiac care unit.

2005

Opened state-of-the-art emergency department and trauma center.

2015

Launched digital health initiatives and telemedicine services.

2024
